Ecommerce is taking over. Learn everything there is to know about starting your own business.

In the past decade, ecommerce has completely disrupted and transformed the retail industry. Hundreds of billions of dollars are spent online every year. From the outside, it may seem that only the largest brands and companies can get a piece of this pie, however now it’s easier than ever to start and manage your own ecommerce business, all from your home. What may start as a side hustle now, can easily turn into a full- time career. With little to no risk getting started, there’s no better time to start than now! Across this website, you will receive the most essential information I’ve picked up after over three years of experience in the ecommerce industry, creating and managing numerous ecommerce brands. But before we get started… you need an idea!

Choose a niche

A niche, or the target audience of your business is a very important step in deciding what kind of product you’re going to sell. When choosing a niche, make sure the audience isn’t too small, but also make sure it’s something you’re knowledgeable about. This will make advertising and marketing much easier, as you will know your audience and product well

Choose a product

Now that you’ve decided on your niche, start brainstorming product ideas that relate to your niche. A great place to look for potential products is AliExpress. AliExpress has thousands of products to choose from, and almost all will be easy to fulfill using any of fulfillment strategies provided on this website

Create your website

Finally, it’s time to create your website. There are numerous different ecommerce services, but the easily the most popular service is Shopify. Shopify is perfect for businesses of any size and provides industry level tools and insights at an affordable price. When designing your website, you can get as detailed as possible using HTML and CSS or use one of their free or paid templates if that isn’t your speed.

Now that you’ve got the basics down, it’s time to learn about marketing.
